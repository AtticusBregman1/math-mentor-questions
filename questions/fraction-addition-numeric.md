Evaluate and simplify: $\frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{6}$

Enter your answer as a simplified fraction (e.g. $\frac{5}{12}$).

---
**Solution:** Find a common denominator: $\frac{1}{4} = \frac{3}{12}$ and $\frac{1}{6} = \frac{2}{12}$, so
$$\frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{6} = \frac{3}{12} + \frac{2}{12} = \frac{5}{12}$$
